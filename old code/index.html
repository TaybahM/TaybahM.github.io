About Me

Sometimes I analyze random logos on billboards, sometimes I overthink simple designs. Sometimes I'll look at the starbucks logo and explain to my unexpected friend why it's not completely symmetrical. Design has always fascinated me and pushed me to learn more and explore different aspects and varities of the craft.

Currently based in Dallas, Texas (aka yeehaw land) to further study Mechanichal/Software Engineering along with my Arabic studies.
