Anchorheart Project

Anchoheart is a remote from home start up volunteer project. It’s a virtual support network that provides individualized one on one support. Our mission is to promote connection, community, end loneliness and isolation. Anchorheart support buddies are caring individuals that provide virtual non proffesional individualized emotional support and companionship to clients.

What to Know

Anchorheart was a project I worked on extensively throughout the month of August in 2022. I was asked to provide brand design and marketing content as soon as possible to get the project up and running. In the short amount of time I had, I was able to create a working website, along with promotional content, forms, business cards, printables and much more. The intensity and short time to complete the project allowed me to progress in my communication and design skills.
