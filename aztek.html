AzteK

AzteK is a mock project I worked on recently. I created this brand to improve my product design skills and test my design abilities. AzteK is a technology company thats base platform is a mobile application. The design was inspired by one of my students who had the initials "AZ". After playing around with the letters, I came up with a smooth way to turn it into a minimal logo.
